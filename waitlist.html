The real cost of context switching

Every time you leave what you're reading to ask a question, you lose momentum. You open a new tab. You rephrase your thought. You wait for an answer. Then you reconstruct where you were.

That's not thinking. That's managing interruptions.

Centralization isn't clarity

Most tools want to be your workspace. They want you to bring everything to them—your documents, your notes, your attention. Tangent does the opposite.

It appears when summoned. It answers in place. It disappears when dismissed.

What Tangent is

Tangent is AI that lives on every page you open. Select text. Choose a question. Get an answer without leaving. No tabs. No context switching. No central hub demanding your workflow conform to its structure.

Who this is for

  • People who read deeply and need answers without breaking focus
  • People suspicious of tools that demand attention
  • People who value autonomy over automation

Who this is not for

  • People looking for an all-in-one workspace
