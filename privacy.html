TANGENT Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

In Short: TANGENT is an AI assistant that helps you understand web content. We collect the page content you're viewing and your questions to provide AI-powered responses via OpenAI. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what's necessary to provide our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Page Content

When you use TANGENT features (selecting text, using chips, or asking questions):

  • Selected text - The text you highlight on web pages
  • Page context - Up to 50,000 characters from the current page (title, headings, paragraphs around your selection)
  • Page URL and metadata - The website address and basic page information

Why we collect this: To provide AI-powered answers that are relevant to the content you're reading.

Where it goes: Your page content is sent to our backend server, then to OpenAI's API to generate responses. We do not permanently store your page content or browsing history.

1.2 User Prompts and AI Responses

  • Your questions - Chat messages and chip queries you submit
  • AI responses - The answers generated by OpenAI

Storage: Prompts and responses are processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers. OpenAI may retain data according to their privacy policy.

1.3 Account Information

  • Email address - Collected when you create an account via Supabase Auth
  • Authentication tokens - Supabase JWT tokens stored in your browser (chrome.storage.sync)

Why we collect this: To manage your account, track your usage quota, and enable subscription features.

1.4 Usage Data

  • Token usage - Number of AI tokens consumed per request
  • Request timestamps - When you make queries
  • Endpoint usage - Which features you use (chat, image analysis, etc.)
  • User ID - Your unique Supabase user identifier

Why we collect this: To enforce usage quotas, prevent abuse, and improve our service.

Retention: Usage logs are stored for 90 days, then automatically deleted.

1.5 Payment Information

  • Billing details - Email, name, and payment method (processed by Stripe)

Important: We never see or store your credit card numbers. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe.

1.6 User Preferences

  • Settings - Your chip layout, theme, language, and shortcuts

Storage: Stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.sync (syncs across your Chrome profile).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI assistance - Send your prompts and page content to OpenAI to generate responses
  • Manage quotas - Track your token usage and enforce subscription limits
  • Process payments - Handle subscriptions via Stripe
  • Prevent abuse - Rate limiting and security monitoring
  • Improve service quality - Analyze usage patterns (aggregated, anonymous data only)
  • Communicate with you - Send account-related emails (quota warnings, subscription updates)

3. Third Parties We Share Data With

3.1 OpenAI

What we share: Your prompts, selected text, and page content

Why: To generate AI-powered responses using GPT-4o

Their policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

Data retention: OpenAI may retain data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring (unless you're on their zero-retention plan)

3.2 Supabase

What we share: Email, user profiles, usage logs, quotas, subscription records

Why: Database and authentication infrastructure

Their policy: Supabase Privacy Policy

Data location: Hosted on AWS (specify region if known)

3.3 Stripe

What we share: Email and subscription metadata

Why: Payment processing for subscriptions and token packs

Their policy: Stripe Privacy Policy

Note: Stripe handles all credit card information directly; we never see your card numbers

4. Data Security

We implement multiple security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption - All data transmitted over secure connections
  • Authentication - Supabase JWT tokens with Row Level Security (RLS)
  • Sensitive data redaction - Credit cards, SSNs, passwords, and API keys are automatically filtered from logs
  • Rate limiting - Prevents spam and abuse
  • Restricted domains - TANGENT is automatically disabled on banking, financial, and government websites
  • XSS protection - All AI output is sanitized with DOMPurify before rendering

5. What We DO NOT Do

  • ❌ We do not sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • ❌ We do not track your browsing across websites for advertising
  • ❌ We do not store browsing history - only usage statistics (token counts, timestamps)
  • ❌ We do not inject ads or affiliate links into web pages
  • ❌ We do not share data with anyone except the third parties listed above (OpenAI, Supabase, Stripe)

6. Data Retention

  • Page content & prompts: Not permanently stored (processed in real-time only)
  • Usage logs: 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Payment records: Retained by Stripe for tax/legal compliance (typically 7 years)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - Request a copy of your account information and usage logs
  • Delete your account - Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data - Download your settings and preferences
  • Opt-out - Uninstall the extension at any time to stop data collection
  • Update preferences - Control which features you use (e.g., disable specific chips)

To exercise these rights: Email us at privacy@tangentapp.co or use the account deletion feature in the extension settings.

8. Children's Privacy

TANGENT is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

TANGENT is operated in the United States. If you're using TANGENT from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using TANGENT, you consent to this transfer.

10. Cookies and Tracking

TANGENT does not use cookies for tracking. We only use browser storage (chrome.storage.sync) to save your preferences and authentication tokens locally on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • If changes are significant, we'll notify you via email or an in-extension notice
  • Your continued use of TANGENT after changes constitutes acceptance
