Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

In Short: TANGENT is an AI assistant that helps you understand web content. We collect the page content you're viewing and your questions to provide AI-powered responses via OpenAI. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what's necessary to provide our service. We do not sell your data, track your browsing for ads, or store your browsing history.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Page Content

When you use TANGENT features (selecting text, using chips, or asking questions):

  • Selected text – The text you highlight on web pages
  • Page context – Up to 50,000 characters from the current page (title, headings, paragraphs around your selection)
  • Page URL and metadata – The website address and basic page information

Why we collect this: To provide AI-powered answers that are relevant to the content you're reading.

Where it goes: Your page content is sent to our backend server, then to OpenAI's API to generate responses. We do not permanently store your page content or browsing history.

1.2 User Prompts and AI Responses

  • Your questions – Chat messages and chip queries you submit
  • AI responses – The answers generated by OpenAI

Storage: Prompts and responses are processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers. OpenAI may retain data according to their privacy policy.

1.3 Account Information

  • Email address – Collected when you create an account via Supabase Auth
  • Authentication tokens – Supabase JWT tokens stored in your browser (chrome.storage.sync)

Why we collect this: To manage your account, track your usage quota, and enable subscription features.

1.4 Usage Data

  • Token usage – Number of AI tokens consumed per request
  • Request timestamps – When you make queries
  • Endpoint usage – Which features you use (chat, image analysis, etc.)
  • User ID – Your unique Supabase user identifier

Why we collect this: To enforce usage quotas, prevent abuse, and improve our service.

Retention: Usage logs are stored for 90 days, then automatically deleted.

1.5 Payment Information

  • Billing details – Email, name, and payment method (processed by Stripe)

Important: We never see or store your credit card numbers. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe.

1.6 User Preferences

  • Settings – Your chip layout, theme preferences, language, and keyboard shortcuts

Storage: Stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.sync (syncs across your Chrome profile).

1.7 User Feedback

  • Feedback submissions – Category, description, email (optional), page URL, extension version, browser info

Why we collect this: To improve our service based on user reports and feature requests.

Retention: Feedback is stored for 2 years, then automatically deleted.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI assistance – Send your prompts and page content to OpenAI to generate responses
  • Manage quotas – Track your token usage and enforce subscription limits
  • Process payments – Handle subscriptions via Stripe
  • Prevent abuse – Rate limiting and security monitoring
  • Improve service quality – Analyze usage patterns (aggregated, anonymous data only)
  • Communicate with you – Send account-related emails (quota warnings, subscription updates, feedback responses)

3. Third Parties We Share Data With

3.1 OpenAI

What we share: Your prompts, selected text, and page content

Why: To generate AI-powered responses using GPT-4o and GPT-4o-mini

Their policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

Data retention: OpenAI may retain data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring

3.2 Supabase

What we share: Email, user profiles, usage logs, quotas, subscription records, feedback submissions

Why: Database and authentication infrastructure

Their policy: Supabase Privacy Policy

Data location: Hosted on AWS in the United States

3.3 Stripe

What we share: Email and subscription metadata

Why: Payment processing for subscriptions and token packs

Their policy: Stripe Privacy Policy

Note: Stripe handles all credit card information directly; we never see your card numbers

3.4 Resend (Optional)

What we share: Your email address and feedback content (only when you submit feedback)

Why: To send email notifications to our support team about your feedback

Their policy: Resend Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We implement multiple security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption – All data transmitted over secure connections
  • Authentication – Supabase JWT tokens with Row Level Security (RLS) on all database tables
  • Sensitive data redaction – Credit cards, SSNs, passwords, and API keys are automatically filtered from logs
  • Rate limiting – Prevents spam and abuse (token bucket algorithm per user/endpoint)
  • Restricted domains – TANGENT is automatically disabled on 43 sensitive websites including banks, government sites, healthcare portals (HIPAA compliance), and legal services (attorney-client privilege protection)
  • XSS protection – All AI output is sanitized with DOMPurify before rendering (Grade A security audit)
  • Regular security audits – Comprehensive security reviews completed December 2025

5. What We DO NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not track your browsing across websites for advertising
  • We do not store browsing history – only usage statistics (token counts, timestamps)
  • We do not inject ads or affiliate links into web pages
  • We do not share data with anyone except the third parties listed above (OpenAI, Supabase, Stripe, Resend)
  • We do not use cookies for tracking – only browser storage for your preferences

6. Data Retention

  • Page content & prompts: Not permanently stored (processed in real-time only)
  • Usage logs: 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • User feedback: 2 years, then automatically deleted
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Inactive accounts (3+ years with no usage) are automatically deleted
  • Payment records: Retained by Stripe for 7 years for tax/legal compliance

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data – Request a copy of your account information and usage logs
  • Export your data – Download all your data as JSON (GDPR Article 20 compliance)
  • Delete your account – Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data (30-day grace period with email confirmation)
  • Cancel deletion – Reverse a deletion request during the 30-day grace period
  • Opt-out – Uninstall the extension at any time to stop data collection
  • Update preferences – Control which features you use (e.g., disable specific chips, adjust settings)

To exercise these rights: Email us at privacy@tangentapp.co or use the account deletion/export features in your account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

TANGENT is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@tangentapp.co.

9. International Users

TANGENT is operated in the United States. If you're using TANGENT from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using TANGENT, you consent to this transfer.

Data location: All data is stored on AWS servers in the United States via Supabase infrastructure.

10. Cookies and Tracking

TANGENT does not use cookies for tracking. We only use browser storage (chrome.storage.sync and chrome.storage.local) to save your preferences, authentication tokens, and chat export data locally on your device. This data syncs across your Chrome profile but never leaves your browser except when you explicitly use TANGENT features.
