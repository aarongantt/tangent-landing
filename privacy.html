Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: TANGENT is committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide our AI-powered browser assistant service. Your conversations are processed solely to generate AI responses and are not used to train AI models or shared with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create a TANGENT account, we collect:

  • Email address – Used for account creation, authentication, and service communications
  • Password – Securely hashed using industry-standard encryption (we never store passwords in plaintext)
  • Trial status – Tracks whether you've used your 7-day free trial
  • Account creation date – For billing cycles and service analytics

1.2 Usage Data

To provide AI-powered assistance, we process:

  • Text selections – Content you select on webpages to analyze with TANGENT
  • Prompts and queries – Your questions and instructions to the AI
  • AI responses – Generated answers stored temporarily for conversation continuity
  • Page context – URL, title, and surrounding text to provide contextual AI responses
  • Browser and extension info – Browser type, extension version (for compatibility and debugging)
  • Token usage metrics – Number of tokens consumed per query (for quota management)

1.3 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or banking details. We receive from Stripe:

  • Stripe Customer ID – Links your account to Stripe
  • Subscription status – Active, canceled, expired
  • Billing cycle dates – For quota resets and renewal reminders
  • Transaction IDs – For billing support and refunds

1.4 Technical and Security Data

For security, fraud prevention, and rate limiting:

  • IP address – Used for rate limiting and fraud detection (not stored long-term)
  • Request timestamps – For rate limiting and abuse prevention
  • Error logs – Anonymous error reports to improve service reliability

1.5 User Feedback

If you submit feedback via the extension:

  • Feedback text – Your comments and suggestions
  • Contact email – Pre-filled if logged in, optional for anonymous feedback
  • Page URL – Page where feedback was submitted (for context)
  • Extension version – For debugging reported issues

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Process AI queries and generate responses using OpenAI's GPT models
  • Maintain conversation context within chat sessions
  • Track quota usage and enforce subscription limits
  • Provide personalized AI assistance based on webpage context

2.2 Account Management

  • Authenticate users and manage sessions
  • Process subscription payments and billing
  • Send service notifications (quota alerts, subscription renewals)
  • Enforce one free trial per email address

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve AI prompt quality
  • Monitor performance and fix bugs
  • Develop new features based on user feedback
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

2.4 What We DO NOT Do

Your data is yours:

  • ❌ We do not sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We do not use your queries to train AI models
  • ❌ We do not share conversations with advertisers
  • ❌ We do not track you across websites (beyond TANGENT usage)

3. Data Retention

3.1 Active Accounts

  • Account data – Retained while your account is active
  • Usage logs – Deleted after 90 days
  • Conversation history – Stored locally on your device, never synced to servers
  • User feedback – Retained for 2 years, then deleted

3.2 Inactive Accounts

  • Accounts with no activity for 3 years are automatically deleted
  • You'll receive email notification 30 days before deletion
  • Subscription records retained for 7 years (tax compliance)

3.3 Account Deletion

  • Request deletion via Account Settings
  • 30-day grace period – Cancel deletion anytime during this window
  • After 30 days, all personal data is permanently deleted
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytics

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Essential Service Providers

We share data only with trusted partners essential to our service:

  • OpenAI – AI model provider. Your queries are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses. OpenAI does not use TANGENT queries to train their models per Enterprise Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase – Database and authentication provider. Stores user accounts, quotas, and usage logs securely.
  • Stripe – Payment processor. Handles all credit card transactions. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.
  • Vercel – Cloud hosting provider. Hosts our backend API functions.
  • Resend – Email delivery service for transactional emails (receipts, notifications).

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect against fraud or security threats
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of TANGENT, our users, or the public

4.3 Business Transfers

If TANGENT is acquired or merged with another company, your data may be transferred. We'll notify you via email before any such transfer and give you options.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification – Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure – Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data portability – Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Restrict processing – Limit how we use your data
  • Object – Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Withdraw consent – Stop data processing based on consent at any time

5.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know – What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Delete – Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out – We do not sell personal information, so no opt-out is necessary
  • Non-discrimination – You won't be penalized for exercising privacy rights

5.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To request data export, deletion, or exercise other rights:

  • Account Settings: tangentapp.co/account
  • Email: privacy@tangentapp.co

We'll respond within 30 days for GDPR requests and 45 days for CCPA requests.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit – All data sent over HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at rest – Database stored with AES-256 encryption
  • Row Level Security (RLS) – Database policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Restricted domains – Extension blocked on banking, government, and healthcare sites
  • AI response sanitization – Automated filtering of SSNs, credit cards, API keys
  • Rate limiting – Prevents abuse and denial-of-service attacks
  • Token expiry – Auth tokens expire and refresh automatically

No system is 100% secure. While we take extensive precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Use TANGENT responsibly and avoid submitting highly sensitive data (passwords, SSNs, credit cards).

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Essential Cookies

  • Authentication token – Keeps you logged in
  • Session ID – Maintains conversation context

7.2 What We Don't Use

  • ❌ No advertising cookies
  • ❌ No cross-site tracking
  • ❌ No behavioral profiling
  • ❌ No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)

8. Children's Privacy

TANGENT is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@tangentapp.co and we'll delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. By using TANGENT, you consent to your data being transferred to these jurisdictions. We ensure adequate protections through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
  • GDPR-compliant data processing agreements with providers
  • Regular security and compliance audits

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes:

  • We'll notify you via email if you're a registered user
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance
