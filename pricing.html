Pricing FAQ

What are queries?

Each chip action or chat message counts as one query. All queries use the same AI models—simple requests are faster, complex requests use more advanced reasoning.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime through your account page. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What happens if I run out of queries?

Your quota resets monthly. Upgrade your plan for more queries or wait for your monthly reset.

Do unused queries roll over?

No. Query quotas reset on your billing anniversary each month.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your account page anytime. Access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Is there a refund policy?

Contact support@tangentapp.co within 7 days for a full refund.
