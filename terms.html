9. Token Quotas and Usage

TANGENT uses a token-based system to measure your usage of AI services.

Token Types

  • Standard Tokens: Used for fast queries powered by GPT-4o-mini (most chips and simple chat messages)
  • Deep Tokens: Used for complex queries powered by GPT-4o (image analysis, deep reasoning, "deep mode" queries)

Token Allocation

  • Tokens are allocated monthly on your subscription reset date (the day of the month you first subscribed)
  • Token Packs add tokens immediately to your account upon purchase
  • Monthly subscription tokens reset each month and do NOT roll over. Unused tokens from your monthly quota are forfeited at the end of each billing cycle.
  • Token Pack tokens persist until used and do not expire
  • When you use TANGENT, subscription tokens are consumed first, then Token Pack tokens

Usage Tracking

  • Your current token balance is displayed in your account dashboard and in the extension
  • We log all AI requests for billing, quota enforcement, and abuse prevention
  • You will receive email notifications when you reach 80% and 100% of your token quota
  • Usage logs include: timestamp, chip or query type, tokens consumed, and model used

Overage Policy

If you exceed your token quota:

  • TANGENT will display a notification prompting you to upgrade your plan or purchase Token Packs
  • We do not automatically charge overage fees
  • You will not be able to use AI features until you add more tokens (by upgrading or purchasing Token Packs) or your quota resets at the start of your next billing cycle
