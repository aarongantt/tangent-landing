Download Tangent

AI-powered browser assistant. Select text, choose a prompt chip, and get instant AI responses on any webpage.

Add to Chrome - It's Free
  1. 1
    Click "Add to Chrome" above to open the Chrome Web Store listing.
  2. 2
    Click "Add to Chrome" in the Web Store, then confirm the installation.
  3. 3
    Select any text on a webpage. The Tangent chip bar will appear automatically.
  4. 4
    Click a chip (Summarize, Explain, Teach Me, etc.) to get an instant AI response.
Requirements: Google Chrome (version 128 or later) on desktop. Firefox and Safari support coming soon.
