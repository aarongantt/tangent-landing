TANGENT - FAQ

Everything you need to know about Tangent, the floating AI assistant that lives directly on every page you open. Find answers about features, security, pricing, and more.

GENERAL OVERVIEW

Tangent is a floating, on-page AI assistant built to support the natural way people think. It helps you stay immersed in the subject in front of you while giving you the freedom to explore any question, tangent, or idea that arises along the way. Instead of switching tabs or copy-pasting text into a chatbot, Tangent appears directly on the webpages you're working on and responds with context-aware intelligence. It becomes part of your workflow—an unobtrusive companion that moves at the speed of your curiosity.

Most great thinking doesn't happen in straight lines. You follow a thought, then suddenly another idea appears, and another. Tangent is designed to support both the deep-focus moments and the spontaneous detours—those creative sparks that turn simple questions into discoveries. It lets you explore those tangents without breaking your flow or losing your place.

A normal chatbot requires you to stop, switch tabs, provide context, and then return to what you were doing. Tangent eliminates all that friction. It understands what you're reading, what text you highlighted, and what problem you're trying to solve. It anchors your thinking directly to the page, making AI feel less like a separate tool and more like a natural extension of your working mind.

HOW TANGENT WORKS

Tangent listens only when you interact with it. When you ask a question or click a prompt chip, it gathers the text you highlighted, the surrounding paragraph, and anything you typed. It sends this structured context to Tangent's secure backend, where your subscription is checked, the right model is selected, and a clean prompt is built for the AI. The backend returns the result, and Tangent displays it directly in the floating mini-chat. Heavy lifting happens in the cloud; the smooth, responsive experience happens in your browser.

All data transmission uses end-to-end encryption, and Tangent's backend does not store your conversations or page content after processing your request.

Tangent currently partners with OpenAI and uses their ChatGPT models (GPT-4o for Deep Mode and GPT-4o-mini for Fast Mode). Our plan is to offer additional model options in the near future, giving you more choice and flexibility in how you work with AI.

Anywhere you want it. When you highlight text, the chip bar appears nearby with smart one-click actions. When you want a deeper interaction, the mini-chat window opens—draggable, resizable, and always ready to help. It lives above the page, not inside it, so your original workflow stays untouched.

The mini-chat is your on-page conversational workspace. It's where you ask questions about what you're reading, workshop ideas, refine writing, compare concepts, or unravel complicated material. It keeps everything close to the source material, letting you explore without leaving the page. Think of it as a small companion that grows with your thought process.

Fast Mode is Tangent at full speed—quick, responsive, great for summaries, clarifications, rewrites, definitions, or short answers. It uses a highly efficient model (GPT-4o-mini) so it feels instant and lightweight, perfect for the dozens of small interactions you have throughout your day.
