TANGENT - FAQ

Everything you need to know about Tangent, the floating AI assistant that lives directly on every page you open. Find answers about features, security, pricing, and more.

GENERAL OVERVIEW

Tangent is a floating, on-page AI assistant built to support the natural way people think. It helps you stay immersed in the subject in front of you while giving you the freedom to explore any question, tangent, or idea that arises along the way. Instead of switching tabs or copy-pasting text into a chatbot, Tangent appears directly on the webpages you're working on and responds with context-aware intelligence. It becomes part of your workflow—an unobtrusive companion that moves at the speed of your curiosity.

Most great thinking doesn't happen in straight lines. You follow a thought, then suddenly another idea appears, and another. Tangent is designed to support both the deep-focus moments and the spontaneous detours—those creative sparks that turn simple questions into discoveries. It lets you explore those tangents without breaking your flow or losing your place.

A normal chatbot requires you to stop, switch tabs, provide context, and then return to what you were doing. Tangent eliminates all that friction. It understands what you're reading, what text you highlighted, and what problem you're trying to solve. It anchors your thinking directly to the page, making AI feel less like a separate tool and more like a natural extension of your working mind.

HOW TANGENT WORKS

Tangent listens only when you interact with it. When you ask a question or click a prompt chip, it gathers the text you highlighted, the surrounding paragraph, and anything you typed. It sends this structured context to Tangent's secure backend, where your subscription is checked, the right model is selected, and a clean prompt is built for the AI. The backend returns the result, and Tangent displays it directly in the floating mini-chat. Heavy lifting happens in the cloud; the smooth, responsive experience happens in your browser.

All data transmission uses end-to-end encryption, and Tangent's backend does not store your conversations or page content after processing your request.

Tangent currently partners with OpenAI and uses their ChatGPT models (GPT-4o for Deep Mode and GPT-4o-mini for Fast Mode). Our plan is to offer additional model options in the near future, giving you more choice and flexibility in how you work with AI.

Anywhere you want it. When you highlight text, the chip bar appears nearby with smart one-click actions. When you want a deeper interaction, the mini-chat window opens—draggable, resizable, and always ready to help. It lives above the page, not inside it, so your original workflow stays untouched.

The mini-chat is your on-page conversational workspace. It's where you ask questions about what you're reading, workshop ideas, refine writing, compare concepts, or unravel complicated material. It keeps everything close to the source material, letting you explore without leaving the page. Think of it as a small companion that grows with your thought process.

Deep Dive is your way of saying, "Let's take this further." When you tap it, Tangent carries your entire conversation—your selection, context, instructions, and history—into a full-size ChatGPT window. That expanded space allows for longer, more detailed reasoning, creative development, or multi-step exploration.

GETTING STARTED

Installing Tangent is simple. Visit the Download page, click "Add to Chrome" (or your Chromium browser), and the extension will install automatically. Once installed, you'll see the Tangent icon in your browser toolbar. Click it to sign in or create an account, and you're ready to start.

Yes. Tangent requires an account to manage your subscription, token usage, and settings. You can sign up for a free 7-day trial with no credit card required. After the trial, you'll need to choose a paid plan to continue using Tangent.

Tangent comes with several built-in Prompt Chips like "Explain," "Summarize," and "Translate." To create your own custom chip, open Tangent's settings (click the extension icon, then Settings), navigate to the "Prompts" tab, and click "Create Custom Chip." Enter your prompt text, give it a name, and it will appear in your chip bar when you select text.

Start simple: visit any article or webpage, highlight a paragraph, and click "Explain" or "Summarize" from the chip bar. This will open the mini-chat window with Tangent's response. From there, you can ask follow-up questions, refine your understanding, or explore related ideas. The more you use it, the more natural it becomes.

TOKEN SYSTEM & BILLING

Tangent offers two subscription tiers to fit different usage levels. All prices and plans are available on our Pricing page:

  • Pro: $10/month or $105/year ($8.75/mo) — 12M standard tokens, 750K deep tokens per month
  • Business Solo: $29/month or $290/year ($24.17/mo) — 20M standard tokens, 2M deep tokens per month

Need more tokens? One-time token packs are available: Small Pack (1M standard, 250K deep), Medium Pack (5M standard, 1.25M deep), Large Pack (20M standard, 5M deep).

Your plan includes two monthly token pools: standard tokens for everyday tasks and deep tokens for complex reasoning and image analysis. Each pool resets automatically every month. When a pool runs out, Tangent simply pauses the tasks that use that pool and lets you decide whether to upgrade or add more tokens.

Because not all thinking requires the same computational effort. Most tasks—summaries, explanations, small edits—are inexpensive and happen in Fast Mode. More demanding tasks—like big reasoning jumps or interpreting images—use Deep Mode. By separating the pools, Tangent gives you freedom in lightweight moments and control in heavyweight ones.

Tangent never surprises you with overages. You'll receive notifications when you're approaching your limit (at 80% and 90%). When you hit your limit, you can:

  • Wait until your next monthly reset (date shown in your account dashboard)
  • Upgrade to a higher tier (prorated pricing applies automatically)
  • Purchase one-time token packs from your account page

If standard tokens are exhausted, Fast Mode pauses. If deep tokens run out, Deep Mode and image analysis pause. It's entirely up to you how to proceed.

SECURITY & PRIVACY

Yes. Tangent uses a security-first design. Your browser never stores or sends OpenAI API keys—only Tangent's backend communicates with the model. All communication is encrypted, Supabase enforces strong row-level security, and sensitive information is automatically redacted on your device before leaving your browser.

No. Tangent sends only what's necessary to answer your request: the text you highlighted, the message you typed, and a small portion of surrounding context. It does not send full pages, full HTML, unseen content, or background browsing data.

Tangent sends the text you process through our backend to OpenAI's API to generate responses. According to OpenAI's standard data usage policy, conversation data sent through the API may be used to improve their service. Tangent itself does not use your data to train models.

Your chat conversations are stored locally in your browser only and are automatically cleared when your browser session ends. We log only usage metadata (tokens consumed, models used, timestamps) for billing purposes—not the actual content of your conversations.

Tangent automatically turns itself off on sensitive sites. When you visit a banking portal, medical platform, tax filing site, or other page containing sensitive input fields, Tangent stays inactive unless you explicitly choose to allow it. This protects you from accidentally sending private information anywhere.

Tangent uses a combination of a curated domain list and detection of sensitive form fields—like credit card numbers, SSNs, account numbers, patient info, or government IDs. If any of these signals appear, Tangent disables itself and alerts you.

Yes. Before any content leaves your browser, Tangent performs automatic redaction. It masks SSNs, credit card numbers, bank routing numbers, email addresses, phone numbers, and unique identifiers to safeguard your privacy.

No. Tangent does not access password fields or credential stores. If a page has password fields, Tangent disables itself and waits for your permission before doing anything.

Only the essentials: your question, your highlighted text, and a small, trimmed slice of nearby context—after redaction. Tangent does not capture or transmit full webpages, browsing history, or off-page information.

When you cancel your subscription, we immediately set your token quota to zero, preventing further API usage. Your account profile and historical usage data (metadata like tokens consumed and timestamps—not conversation content) are retained for compliance and business records purposes.

Chat history stored locally in your browser is not affected by cancellation and remains under your control. If you want complete account deletion for GDPR or privacy reasons, please contact support and we'll process your request promptly.

ACCOUNT & CONTROL

You control where and how Tangent operates. You can disable it globally, disable it per site, allow it once or always on sensitive pages, clear local data, or uninstall the extension entirely. Tangent never overrides your choices.

Your chat history is stored locally in your browser during your session and persists until you close the browser or clear your data. Tangent does not upload or sync your conversation history to the cloud unless you manually save or export them. You can clear your local history at any time from the settings.
